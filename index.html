▶ Frequently Asked Questions

How do online sessions work?

All sessions are conducted via video call using platforms like Zoom or Google Meet. I use interactive digital whiteboards, screen sharing, and real-time collaboration tools to make lessons engaging and effective. It's just like being in the same room!

What if my child doesn't connect with your teaching style?

I adapt my teaching approach to match each student's learning style. If needed, I can adjust my methods or recommend alternative resources. Your satisfaction is guaranteed within the first 30 days.

Do you provide materials and resources?

Yes! I provide digital worksheets, practice problems, study guides, and access to online learning tools. All materials are included in your package at no extra cost.

How do you track student progress?

I maintain detailed records of each session, track assignment scores, and provide weekly progress reports. Parents receive regular updates on their child's improvement and areas needing attention.

What's your cancellation policy?

Sessions can be rescheduled with 24-hour notice at no charge. Emergency cancellations are handled case-by-case. I understand life happens and am flexible with scheduling.

Do you offer group sessions?

Yes! Group sessions (2-4 students) are available at discounted rates. Perfect for siblings, friends studying the same subject, or study groups preparing for the same exam.
