How do online English lessons work?

All sessions are conducted via video call using platforms like Zoom or Google Meet. I use interactive whiteboards for grammar lessons, screen sharing for reading exercises, and real-time document collaboration for writing practice. It's just like having a personal English teacher right beside you!

Can you help with my specific English goals?

Absolutely! Whether you need help with academic writing, IELTS/TOEFL preparation, business English, or conversation practice, I customize every lesson to match your specific goals and current level.

Do you provide study materials and resources?

Yes! I provide worksheets, writing templates, vocabulary lists, grammar guides, and practice tests. All materials are included in your package. I also recommend books and online resources based on your interests.

How do you track my English progress?

I maintain detailed records of your strengths and areas for improvement, track your writing progress, and provide regular feedback. You'll receive progress reports showing your improvement in speaking, writing, reading, and listening skills.

What if I'm too shy to speak English?

Many of my students start feeling shy about speaking! I create a comfortable, judgment-free environment where making mistakes is part of learning. We'll start with low-pressure activities and gradually build your confidence.

Do you offer IELTS/TOEFL preparation?

Yes! I'm certified in IELTS preparation and have helped hundreds of students achieve their target scores. I provide practice tests, strategies for each section, and personalized feedback to maximize your score.
